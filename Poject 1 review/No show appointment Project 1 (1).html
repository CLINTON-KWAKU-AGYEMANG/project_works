Introduction¶

In this project I will be doing an analysis on the "No show appoinment dataset". Data was collected on the question of whether or not patients show up for their medical appointment they booked in a hospital in Brazil. The dataset contains information of 100,000 medical appointments from patients with different attribute of disease.

These questions will be analysed;

  1. What is the relation between the age and the no-show column?
  2. What is the distribution of patients between scholarship and disease?
  3. What is the Gender distribution of the Scholarship among the patients?.
